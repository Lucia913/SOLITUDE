DESIGN DETAILS

设计细节

Person and device

人与装置

A problem was raised on the exterior wall of the installation, and one could choose one of the entrances. No matter which entrance you enter, you will eventually come out through another door.
装置的外墙上提出了问题，人们可以选择其中一个入口进去。无论从哪个入口进去最终都会从另一个门出来。

Just after entering the device, you will see yourself in the mirror. People can follow the tips on the mirror to better reflect on themselves.
刚进入装置后会看到镜子里的自己，人们可以根据镜子上的提示进行接下来的动作，更好的进行自我反思。 

The setting of this small dark space is to help people think. Referring to the way of thinking in ancient times, people can try to think in this space.
这个黑暗的小空间的设置是为了帮助人们进行思考，参考了古代人们面壁思过的思考方式，人们可以尝试着在这个空间里进行思考。 

There's a wall here with more questions for people to answer to help them reflect on themselves. People can use the clay on the table to squeeze out their own mind of themselves, or they can knead casually to help thinking.
这里的一面墙上有着更多的问题供人们回答，来帮助他们自我反思。人们可以用桌上的陶土捏出自己心目中的自己，或者可以随便捏捏帮助思考。 

Interaction details

交互细节

①

Handwriting and ideas from the previous person
前一个人留下的笔迹和想法

②

People erase the previous handwriting after reading
人们在阅读后擦除前一个留下的笔迹

③

Write your thoughts with an erasable marker
运用可擦除的马克笔写下自己的思考

This is about the details of the mirror. Using an erasable marker, the person entering the device can read the handwriting left by the previous person, and after erasing, he can leave his own thoughts. Other people's reflection framework can help people better reflect on themselves.
这是关于镜子的细节，运用了可以擦除的马克笔，进入装置的人可以先阅读上一个人留下的笔迹，擦除后可以留下自己的想法。在他人的反思框架中可以帮助人们更好的反思自己。

①

Read and erase the previous handwriting
阅读并擦除前一个留下的笔迹

②

Write your thoughts with an erasable marker
运用可擦除的马克笔写下自己的思考

③

You can use clay to help you think
可以通过捏陶泥帮助思考

The walls here are also processed to erase the handwriting left by the previous person and write their own ideas. The clay on the table presents the self in the eyes of others. People can pinch themselves out again or modify it. The table will show the clay that has been modified many times. People can also observe the existing clay before kneading it. It is also a very interesting thing to observe oneself in the eyes of others.
这里墙面也是经过处理的，同样可以擦除前一个人留下的笔迹并写出自己的想法。桌上的陶土呈现的是别人眼中的自己，人们可以重新捏出自己也可以对其进行修改。桌上就会呈现进行很多次修改的陶土，人们也可以在捏之前先观察已有的陶土，观察别人眼中的自己也是一件很有意思的事。
