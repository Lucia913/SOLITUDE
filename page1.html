独处 · 自省

Solitude ＆ Self-reflection

INTRODUCTION

作品介绍

In our daily life, we have many social roles. In the transformation of different roles, we will feel tired and even lose ourselves. Sometimes, we need to get away from dealing with complex relationships and have our own time and space.
在日常生活中我们有很多社会角色，在不同角色的转换中我们会感到疲惫，甚至会迷失自我。有时候，我们需要从处理复杂人际关系中抽离，拥有属于自己的时间和空间。 

My design is mainly to provide a space for people to be alone and guide people to reflect in this space. In the continuous cycle of asking questions and the help of various facilities, let people talk to themselves, calm down and listen to their inner voice, so that people can understand themselves better and learn to get along with themselves.
我的这个设计主要是想给人们提供一个独处空间，引导人们在这个空间里进行反思。在不断的循环发问和各种设施的帮助中让人们与自己对话、安静下来聆听内心的声音，使人们更了解自己、学会与自己相处。
