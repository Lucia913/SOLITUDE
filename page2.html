DESIGN CONCEPT

设计概念

Research process

调研过程

Through the interview, I found that most of the interviewees aged 19-25 choose to relax, release pressure or self reflection when they are alone, but most of them do not know what to think and how to reflect in this space, so I want to design this product as a space for self relaxation, listening to their own heart, self reflection and self thinking.
通过采访我发现年龄在19-25的被采访者在独处时大多数会选择放松自己、释放压力或者自我反思，但他们中的大多数都不知道应该在这个空间里思考什么、如何反思。所以我想把这个产品设计为一个自我放松，聆听自己内心和自我反思、自我思考的空间。 

User analysis

用户分析


Consumer
用户人群 
The users are mainly college students (young people aged 19-25). At this age, most people are very energetic, like to push forward, but rarely reflect on themselves. At the same time, in a brand-new environment, it is easy to lose yourself. I think these groups need to reflect, understand themselves, and learn to listen to their inner voice.
用户主要集中在大学生（年龄在19-25的青年人）。在这个年纪的人们大多很有干劲，喜欢努力向前冲，却很少反思自己。同时在一个崭新的环境里也会面临着各种压力，很容易迷失自我。我认为这些群体都需要反思、了解自己，学会倾听自己内心的声音。

Place of use
使用场所
At present, the use of the place is in the University, the product as a non mobile device placed in the university campus for students to be alone and reflect. In the future, it will be placed in the exhibition hall for user research, and my vision for it is mobile and multi age oriented. 
在目前，使用的场所是在大学里，这个产品作为一个不能移动的装置放置在大学校园里供学生进行独处和反思。在未来将会把它放在在展览馆进行用户调研，我对它的设想是可移动的、面向多个年龄层次的。 

Illusion of choice

选择的幻觉


People are faced with different choices before entering the device, but no matter which entrance they choose to enter, they will always go out through another door. The setting of the questions raised in the device is also a cycle, allowing people to move forward in constant self questioning and self reflection. The mirror in the device can create visual illusion to a certain extent, and it can also reflect the illusion of choice in the door access and problem cycle.
人们进入这个装置之前面临着不同的选择，但无论选择从哪个入口进入，总会从另外一个门出去。装置里对于提出的问题的设置也是一个循环，让人们在不停的自我提问、自我反思中前进。装置内设置的镜子在一定程度上可以制造视觉上的幻觉，在门的进出和问题的循环中也可以体现出选择的幻觉。
