Problem cycle

问题循环


This solitude device can only enter one person at a time. Before entering the device, people will see the question "do you know yourself?" people can enter different entrances according to their answers. After entering the device, people answer a series of questions, and the facilities in the device help to think, so that people can listen to their inner voice, learn to get along with themselves, conduct self reflection, and let themselves know themselves better.
这个独处装置每次只可以进入一人，进入这个装置前人们将会看到“你了解自己吗”这个问题，人们可以根据自己的回答进入不同的入口。在进入装置后，人们通过回答一系列问题，装置内的设施帮助思考，使人们倾听自己内心的声音、学会与自己相处、进行自我反思、让自己更加了解自己。
